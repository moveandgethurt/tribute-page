Leopold Bloom

The man who ate the inner organs of beasts and fowls

Here's a timeline of Mr. Leopold Bloom's life in Ulysses

  • 1866 - born in Dublin, Ireland, to Rudolph Bloom (a Hungarian Jew) and Ellen Higgins (an Irish Protestant)
  • 1888 - converted to Catholicism to marry Marion (Molly) Tweedy
  • 1889 - Millicent (Milly) Bloom is born, their only daughter
  • 1893 - their son Rudolph is born, died after 11 days
  • 1904 - takes a walk around Dublin:
    1. goes to the post office
    2. pharmacy (to buy soap)
    3. the Freeman's Journal office
    4. Graham Lemon's candy shop
    5. Davy Byrne's pub
    6. National Library of Ireland
    7. Ormond Hotel
    8. Barney Kiernan's pub
    9. maternity hospital
    10. Bella Cohen's brothel
    11. cabman's shelter
    12. to eventually return home at 7 Eccles Street

He liked thick giblet soup, nutty gizzards, a stuffed roast heart, liverslices fried with crustcrumbs, fried hencods' roes. But most of all, he liked grilled mutton kidneys which gave to his palate a fine tang of faintly scented urine.

-- James Joyce, Ulysses
